Introduction

Welcome to AegisNet!
Developed by Nusla Ismail as part of a final-year project, AegisNet is a cutting-edge fraud detection platform powered by the revolutionary GAT-enhanced Dynamic Graph Neural Network (DGNN) model. Designed to tackle the complexities of financial fraud, AegisNet combines advanced AI with real-time analytics to uncover hidden patterns and safeguard financial systems. Experience the future of financial security with AegisNet!

How to use

Click on Analyze to upload a CSV file. Press Detect Fraud to run the fraud analysis.
Generated fraud graphs will be saved and accessible under the Graphs section.
